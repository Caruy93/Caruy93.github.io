About me

I am a developer based in Helsinki, originally from Melbourne, Australia.

I enjoy making digital things, be it websites, applications, or games! My passion is in delivering delightful experiences that bring people joy, and perfecting the subtle details that make a great product.

After graduating from Monash University with honours, I joined RBB Economics where I worked as consultant and data specialist. Since moving to Finland, I have focused on working as an independent developer, nurturing my interest in design.
